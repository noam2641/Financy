## FINDINGS
- **A5-F01** Cost model is entry-centric/one-sided; round-trip status undefined. Grade A (spec-internal) · HIGH · REQ-COST-001, REQ-DEC-001, REQ-LAB-001 · blocks release_0_definition, release_0_acceptance. One `open_auction_cost_bps`, no exit/close-auction field; a single cost subtraction; but the label round trip crosses the spread and pays impact twice and touches two liquidity events. If one-way (natural reading), modeled net edge overstated by ≈ a full one-way cost.
- **A5-F02** Always-fill-at-open optimism; limit-at-open adverse selection unmodeled at the gate. Grade A/B · HIGH · REQ-LAB-002, REQ-FILL-003/004/005 · blocks release_1A, paper. Filled subset is negatively selected (fills when it opens weak, misses favorable gaps). Spec has the right instrument (`*_if_filled_h`) but R1 may omit a learned fill model → R1A value-proof read off optimistic all-candidate IC.
- **A5-F03** Fixed-horizon research label ≠ barrier/stop/time-stop realized exit → IC does not map to executable PnL. Grade A · HIGH · REQ-LAB-006/001 vs REQ-SCH-019 · blocks release_0_acceptance, release_1A. Ranking target path-independent; realized execution path-dependent. Spec separates research/policy/realized returns (credit) but the ranked object is the research label; no requirement forces IC→post-barrier-PnL concordance at the gate.
- **A5-F04** Gap-through-stop tail: sizing formula concentrates max notional on max gap risk. Grade A · HIGH · REQ-RISK-002/004 · blocks release_1A, paper, live. `raw_position_notional = risk_budget / planned_stop_distance_pct` → tightest stops get largest notional; REQ-RISK-004 concedes "stop is not guaranteed loss" but `gap_through_stop_sizing_adjustment` undefined and TBD_BLOCKER. 5–14 session holds straddle ≥4–13 overnights; a 14-session window has meaningful probability of straddling earnings; gaps execute at open print not stop price. Correction: size to worst-case overnight/earnings gap, not stop distance; earnings-in-horizon de-rate/flag.
- **A5-F05** No exit/close-auction execution leg modeled (companion to F01/F03). Grade A · MED-HIGH · REQ-SIM-002, REQ-COST-001 · blocks release_0_acceptance. Simulator lists open-auction/partial-fills/slippage but no closing-auction assumption and no exit-cost field.
- **A5-F06** PROXY open-execution quality qualitatively, not quantitatively, specified. Grade A · MED-HIGH · REQ-PRICE-005/006 · blocks release_0_definition, release_0_acceptance. Retail EOD OHLCV lacks true opening-auction clearing prints → PROXY is the default; unquantified "conservative" lets a backtest fill at clean `first_regular_session_bar_open` (most over-optimistic; opening prints have highest intraday spread/imbalance; MOO deviates more than MOC).
- **A5-F07** Capacity/ADV-participation limits entirely TBD; square-root impact unbounded. Grade A/B · HIGH · REQ-RISK-003 (backlog TBD_BLOCKER) · blocks release_1A, paper, live; contaminates release_0_acceptance cost realism. Impact ≈ √(size/ADV) (Almgren exponent ≈0.4–0.6). With no participation cap, per-trade impact unbounded; on small/mid-cap can exceed edge.
- **A5-F08** Partial-fill → stop-quantity atomicity unspecified. Grade A · MED · `LONG_PARTIALLY_FILLED`, REQ-RISK-003, REQ-SIM-002 · blocks paper (R3); should be defined at release_1A. No requirement binds protective-stop quantity to confirmed filled quantity.
- **A5-F09** CONTRADICTORY HYPOTHESIS CONFIRMED (directionally): realistic round-trip cost on the target band plausibly eliminates a thin sector-excess swing edge. Grade B · CRITICAL (to value thesis) · blocks release_0_acceptance, release_1A. Strategy high-turnover (5-session hold ≈ ~100%/week one-way; 14 ≈ ~100%/~3 weeks) — far above Novy-Marx & Velikov ~50%/month threshold above which "few" anomalies retain net spreads; their mid-turnover per-trade cost 20–57 bps. Optimistic counter (Frazzini-Israel-Moskowitz ~6 bps median) is large-cap patient/VWAP institutional, not aggressive MOO/limit-on-open; momentum capacity only ~$2B. A thin gross edge (20–50 bps/trade over 5–14d typical) plausibly erased by a 20–100+ bps round trip; refutable only by a net-of-cost backtest that does not yet exist.
